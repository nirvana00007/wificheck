**Privacy Policy for WiFi Inspector & Scanner**

**Effective Date:** March 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how "WiFi Inspector & Scanner" (the "App") handles information when you use it.

**1. Information We Collect**
The App **does not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information or personal data to our servers or any third parties. All network scanning and data processing occur locally on your device.

**2. Permissions Required**
To function correctly, the App requires certain permissions on your Android device:
*   **Location Permission:** Following Android's security model, accessing detailed WiFi information (such as the SSID and BSSID of your connected network) requires Location permission. We only use this permission to retrieve your network information locally; we do not track your physical location.
*   **Nearby Devices Permission (Android 13+):** Needed specifically to scan and discover nearby WiFi access points without needing location access directly depending on the Android version.
*   **Network & WiFi State:** Required to scan your local network, display connected devices, and fetch nearby WiFi access points securely.

**3. Data Storage**
The App allows you to mark devices on your network as "Known" or "Stranger". This preference data is saved locally on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms (SharedPreferences). It is never uploaded or shared. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

**4. External Links & Third Parties**
The App does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising frameworks that track your behavior.

**5. Changes to This Privacy Policy**
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

**6. Contact Us**
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at [Insert Your Support Email Here].

**《WiFi 检测与扫描器》隐私政策**

**生效日期：** 2026年3月

我们非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策旨在说明“WiFi 检测与扫描器”（以下简称“本应用”）在您使用过程中如何处理相关信息。

**1. 我们收集的信息**
本应用 **不**收集、存储或向我们的服务器及任何第三方传输任何个人身份信息或个人数据。所有的网络扫描和数据处理均在您的设备本地完成。

**2. 所需权限**
为了正常运行，本应用需要获取您 Android 设备上的特定权限：
*   **位置权限：** 根据 Android 系统的安全机制，获取详细的 WiFi 信息（如您所连接网络和周围网络的 SSID 和 BSSID）需要位置权限。我们仅使用该权限在本地读取您的网络状态，绝不会追踪您的实际地理位置。
*   **附近设备权限（Android 13及以上）：** 用于安全扫描和发现附近的 WiFi 接入点。
*   **网络和 WiFi 状态：** 用于扫描您的局域网、显示连接的设备并获取附近网络参数。

**3. 数据存储**
本应用允许您将网络中的设备标记为“已知”或“陌生”。这些偏好数据仅使用 Android 标准的存储机制（SharedPreferences）保存在您的设备本地。我们绝不会上传或共享这些数据。您可以随时通过清除应用数据或卸载本应用来删除这些记录。

**4. 第三方服务**
本应用未集成任何会追踪您行为的第三方分析或广告框架。

**5. 隐私政策的变更**
我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。如有任何修改，我们会在本页面发布新的隐私政策。
